Diet Recommendations for Heart Disease Stages

Diet for Stage 1: Early/Asymptomatic Stage

Healthy Eating for Heart Health

Increase fiber with whole grains, vegetables, and fruits. Choose healthy fats like olive oil, nuts, and seeds. Opt for lean proteins and limit sodium intake.

Diet for Stage 2: Angina (Chest Pain) Stage

Potassium-Rich Foods

Foods high in potassium help regulate blood pressure and support heart function. Include bananas, sweet potatoes, and leafy greens in your meals.

Diet for Stage 3: Heart Attack (Myocardial Infarction) Stage

Low Saturated Fat Diet

Avoid red meats and high-fat dairy. Choose lean proteins, plant-based options, and fatty fish. Include omega-3 rich foods like walnuts and flaxseeds.

Low Sodium Diet

Diet for Stage 5: End-Stage Heart Disease

Easy-to-Digest, High-Nutrient Foods

Include small, frequent meals with soft foods. Use nutrient-dense options like smoothies, soups, and pureed vegetables. Limit fluid, sodium, and potassium if required.
