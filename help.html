How Can We Help You?

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I register for the Heart Health Portal?

To register, click on the "Register" link in the navigation bar and fill out the required information. Once done, you can log in and access all the features of the portal.

2. How do I predict my heart disease risk?

After logging in, navigate to the prediction form, where you will enter some health details. Based on the data, the system will predict the likelihood of heart disease.

3. How can I improve my heart health?

You can improve your heart health by following a heart-healthy diet, engaging in regular exercise, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Visit the "Exercise" and "Diet" sections for more information.
